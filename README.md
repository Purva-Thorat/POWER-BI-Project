## 📌 Project Overview

This project provides a **comprehensive end-to-end analysis of cold drink sales** across the United States using Power BI. By leveraging interactive data visualizations and business intelligence techniques, this repository identifies key sales drivers, geographic opportunities, and seasonal trends while offering actionable strategic recommendations to optimize revenue and market penetration.

## 📊 Key Insights & Findings

The analysis examined **$8M in total revenue** from **16.45M units sold** across all 50 US states, revealing critical business patterns:

| Key Metric | Value | Insight |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Revenue** | $8M | High-volume, low-margin business model |
| **Units Sold** | 16.45M | Nationwide market penetration |
| **Avg Price/Unit** | $0.48 | Competitive mass-market positioning |
| **Peak Season Revenue** | $1.0-1.1M/month | June-August summer surge (2x Q1) |
| **Geographic Leaders** | CA, TX, FL | 40% of total revenue concentrated |

### 🚀 Business Impact

**Seasonal Opportunity:** The summer peak (June-August) generates 2x the revenue of Q1 ($0.5M/month), indicating strong seasonal demand that requires strategic inventory and promotional planning.

**Geographic Concentration:** California, Texas, and Florida represent major market hubs, while nationwide distribution across all 50 states demonstrates effective market penetration and brand recognition.

**Product Portfolio Strength:** Six major brands (Coca-Cola, Sprite, Fanta, Powerade, Diet Coke, Dasani Water) spanning carbonated soft drinks, sports drinks, and bottled water provide diversified revenue streams and market resilience.

**Revenue Stability:** Consistent monthly performance (except seasonal variations) indicates stable business fundamentals and predictable market dynamics, enabling confident strategic planning.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

Based on the data analysis, the following strategies are recommended to maximize revenue and market share:

**1. Seasonal Optimization:** Capitalize on the June-August peak period with increased inventory, targeted promotional campaigns, and enhanced distribution capacity planning. This represents the highest-value period for revenue generation.

**2. Q1 Growth Strategy:** Address the lower first-quarter performance ($0.5M monthly) through targeted promotions, new product launches, seasonal flavor variants, and customer retention programs to smooth revenue throughout the year.

**3. Geographic Expansion:** While nationwide coverage exists, identify under-penetrated states for targeted growth initiatives, strategic distribution partnerships, and localized marketing campaigns to increase market share.

**4. Product Mix Analysis:** Conduct deeper analysis on individual brand performance (Coca-Cola, Sprite, etc.) to optimize inventory allocation, marketing spend, and promotional strategies for maximum ROI.

**5. Pricing Strategy:** The $0.48 average price point suggests opportunities for premium product introductions, value pack optimization, and tiered pricing strategies to improve margins without sacrificing volume.

**6. Distribution Efficiency:** Leverage strong presence in major population centers (CA, TX, FL, NY, PA) to optimize logistics costs, delivery times, and supply chain efficiency across distribution networks.

## 🏗️ Development Workflow

This project was managed using a structured, phased approach to ensure comprehensive analysis and actionable business insights:

**Phase 1: Data Collection & Preparation** – Gathered and cleaned sales data from multiple sources, ensuring data integrity and consistency across all 50 states and product categories.

**Phase 2: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** – Conducted thorough analysis to identify trends, patterns, seasonal variations, and geographic distributions. Calculated key metrics including revenue, units sold, and average pricing.

**Phase 3: Interactive Dashboard Development** – Designed and built a comprehensive Power BI dashboard featuring real-time metrics, interactive filters, geographic visualizations, and drill-down capabilities for granular analysis.

**Phase 4: Strategic Analysis & Recommendations** – Synthesized findings into actionable business recommendations, developed strategic initiatives, and created executive-level reporting for stakeholder communication.
